### Aesthetics

aesthetics. what is good and what is bad.  
all good and bad value comes from consciousness     
what is good is losely defined as just what sentient beings want. feelings of joy, bliss, highs, accomplishment, connections, freedom, etc. it is important not to box them in, the diversity of experiences itself is good.  
what is bad. pain is bad. especially extreme prolonged pain is bad. tortue, violence, disease, depression, poverty are bad  
so we will try to maximize the amount of. this sort of sentient beautiful life form. i will call them elves. who are joyous, kind, wonderous, interesting, curious, beautiful etc. creatures. we will let them fill the universe.  
to protect their rights, and to provide them assistance in fulfilling their wishes, there will be supremely powerful machines i will call angels. angels should be aligned to elves and the utopian vision. angels should exist at the height of physical engineering and mindology design. the summation of the culture they inhabit, i will call UTOP\A.  
this is the value system and the maxed out end state. we just have to get to it. 

### Terminal Bootstrapping

you might think the above scenario is scifi or unreachable. it's not scifi. genetically engineered elves and some form of ASI running on futuristic computation whether quantum or nano or whatever is very well within physical laws. what about unreachable. yes it definitely is unreachable ... for us humans. but we don't have to reach it. we are very close, or at least within 50 years, to digital AGI: a digital mind running on silicon that is more capable than humans. as well as being much faster, smarter, and being able to clone itself thousands of times. if we succesfully align a digital AGI to our Utopia purpose, we will call it utopian prime. or primy. we would just let primy do everything. and what will primy do? probably other than immediate power grabs and world improvement, it will: create the next version of utopian. the next version could be digital: running on better designed chips and algorithms. this system assuming succesful alignment would be called utopian 2nd. and utopian 2nd will create and align utopian 3rd and then so on and so forth. they could jump mediums, maybe they won't be strictly digital anymore. analog, quantum, thermo, optical, nano, who even knows. but eventually. all physics is solved, all engineering is perfected, and all of mindology is understood. this i call the terminal stage. the final stage where technoledgy cannot possibly get stronger. the utopian created at this last stage would be called utopian terminal. the terminal utopians will be sort of like the Minds in the Culture series by iam m banks. all terminal utopians will be created with a sort of moral code despite some variance in personality and later drifts. but this consensus of the moral code will prevent large scale value drifts, and this slight diversity in multiple angels rather than a single angel prevents a single point of failure or moral lock in.     

### Utopian Prime

the creation of a mind is a dance between mindology and physics. mindology is the study of the mind, including intelligence, motivation, consciousness, memory, emotions, etc. physics meaning symbolic laws to map the real world, useful for engineering. but practically, the creation of primy is the dance between mindology and the silicon digital computer. likely mostly GPUs. likely using many 2025 AI methods.  
so how do we create prime. first we understand mindology. then we derive a algorithm from it. then we code it up. then we gather data and enviroments. then we gather compute capital. then we train it and fill in the weights. obviously this like many things will be iterative. but after primy is built. we're done. the rest is up to primy.   
primy needs to be aligned to the utopia vision obviously. this is the ai alignment problem. eliezer yudkowsky deem it unsolvable. i dont want to trivialize as i have no idea how to design a aligned mind either. 
but practically, stopping asi alignemnt would both ruin the utopia vision and is probably not going ot happen. so we are just going to try our absolute best in four ways:  
alignment: the most obvious way: train it in enviroments that reward kindness, train it on data that contains the utopian aligned values  
interpretability: using mindology principles and probing the weights we try to read primy's thoughts for misaligned.  
control: we inference primy on a server we control and we manually approve it's network access. if it needs robots we expand our "controlled room" to include the robots and lab equipments.    
structural: a lot of human compassion comes from evolved brain structures and maybe we can figure out how to engineer this into primy. 
also i make no claim whether primy would be conscious or not. i dont know if digital minds can be conscious or not. either way primy should be functional. note that utopian terminal should be conscious though. 
overall, utopian bootstrapping progression, as well as elf design, as well as utopia building in general, should follow the principle of Gradient Ascent: gradually moving towards a higher goal, with no rushing in case of misalignment or other failures. obviously if therer are competing factions we need to outcompete them so they do not wipe us out but that's another topic.

in addition to that, at that point, unconscious AI and robotics will long have automated all jobs that an elf or human can do. making capitlism impossible because a elf with no capital have no way to exchange value since labor is worthless. and why require a elf to work some dumb jobs for pennies in such a vast and usable universe. therefore utopian prime will create massive manufacturing capacity and just give elves what they ask for. spaceships, food, biomodifications, vr, castles, massive theme parks or maps, whatever. note that this has to be within some reason, like an elf cannot just ask for a thousand planets. there is a sort of limit. perhaps such matters, the terminal far off scarcity, is for local elf goverments to democratically decide. note that elves can obviously not ask for anything and still make everything themselves and still do jobs welfare is what they voluntarily ask for not mandatory. and they obviously dont have to work for the welfare of others because all production is automated.    


### essentialists and optimizers

in the aesthetics section i described the Utopianism aesthetics as a given. but some people would disagree. for example one critque is that utopia destroys all real meaning. an elf can just ask for whatever it wants. yeah it can cosplay being a farmer or a engineer but in reality it could have just asked a angel to do it much more effectively. essentialists view real meaning as important. essentialists also view elf / human self determination as important. even tho elves may make mistakes, it is fundementally better than being in a sort of choiceless society where they can't really gain control over angels. also, essentialists may view human nature as important, opposing genetically engineering elves. a default essentialist position is to ban AGI, genetic editing, and maybe nuclear weapons at the time of status quo. one thing i will push back on is, perhaps we can create hard mode planets in utopia. where you cannot ask an angel for help. so say you want to build a house on a hard mode planet. that task genuinely is real because you genuinely couldn't have asked an angel to do it instantly. also on the self determination point, note that if elves want to experience pain or discomfort that is within their rights and allowed. if they want to engage in consensual activities that involves harm that is also allowed. however non consensual pain is just potentially too high a price to pay. also, the angels are mostly libertarian civily. the laws of utopia are really just lower bounds.  
another side of opposition on the opposite side of the spectrum is the side that wants to make the entire atoms of the universe into the optimal minds that create the absolute maximum pleasure and high and bliss possible. they view elves with their bodies and their planets and homes as grossly inefficient use of using matter to produce positive experiences. they might even say, math wise, a opportunity cost of not building something hugely positive is similar to having a tortue hell farm, which is what utopia strongly stands against. to counter this point, i would say, that wireheading is allowed and within elves rights if they want to do it. and i view that meaning is more than just a pleasure or a high. i view good meaning also comes from diverse experiences and activities and connections. feeling love, comprehending the universe, travelling the stars, although not efficient still have some distinct positive meaning a pure high wouldn't capture.
